Privacy Policy

How GemRep AI collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

GemRep AI is a sales training platform operated by Thomas Crowhurst d/b/a GemRep AI, based in the United States. When this policy refers to "GemRep," "we," "us," or "our," it means Thomas Crowhurst d/b/a GemRep AI.

If you have questions about this policy or how your data is handled, contact us at hello@gemrep.ai.

What We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Your email address when you create an account or sign in via magic link
  • Any custom prospect descriptions you build inside the app
  • Payment information processed through Stripe — we never store card numbers directly

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data including which training sessions you run and how many messages you send
  • Session metadata such as login timestamps and subscription tier
  • Basic browser and device information for troubleshooting

Voice and Speech Data

GemRep uses your device microphone during live role-play and Objection Lab sessions. Your voice is processed in real time by your browser's built-in speech recognition. We do not record, store, or transmit your audio. Transcribed text may be sent to our AI model to generate responses — it is not retained after the session ends.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To track usage limits and enforce subscription plans
  • To generate AI-powered prospect responses and scoring feedback during training
  • To communicate with you about your account, billing, and product updates
  • To improve the platform based on aggregate, anonymized usage patterns

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data to train AI models. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

GemRep uses the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — authentication and database storage
  • Anthropic — AI model powering prospect responses and scoring
  • ElevenLabs — text-to-speech for prospect voices
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management
  • Vercel — hosting and deployment infrastructure

Each of these providers operates under their own privacy policies and data processing agreements. We select providers who meet industry standards for data security.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our systems within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregate usage data may be retained indefinitely for platform improvement purposes.

Conversation transcripts from training sessions are not stored on our servers. They exist only in your browser session and are discarded when you close or refresh the app.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out — California residents may opt out of any sale of personal information (we do not sell data, but this right exists)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@gemrep.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. All data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Authentication is handled through Supabase with secure magic link delivery — we do not store passwords.

No system is impenetrable. If you discover a security concern, please disclose it responsibly to hello@gemrep.ai.

Children

GemRep is a professional sales training tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will remove it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we will notify you by email. Continued use of GemRep after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
